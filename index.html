Cybersecurity Made Simple

CSimplified is a company aimed at making cybersecurity simpler-- everyone should have access to cybersecurity without compromise. We believe that everyone should be able to have a safe presence online, regardless of their exposure to computer science. Young people and the elderly especially tend to have a lack of exposure to online security and an add-on which keeps their data secure online while targeting their needs specifically, thereby allowing them to navigate the internet safely with ease.
